Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

🔒 The short version: We collect only what's needed to run the service. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo (when you sign up or connect Google)
  • Content you create: Projects, notes, canvas nodes, and uploaded media
  • Usage data: Basic analytics to improve the platform (page views, feature usage)
  • Device data: Browser type and IP address for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Kexo AI service
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To enable collaboration features (invites, shared projects)
  • To send essential service communications (account changes, security alerts)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore) and Cloudinary for media files. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: Firebase, Cloudinary (essential for the service to function)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent: For any other purpose, only with your explicit permission

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use Firebase Authentication cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your project data in JSON format

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Kexo AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the platform.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@kexoai.com.
