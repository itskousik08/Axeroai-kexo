Features

Everything you need to think better

A complete toolkit for visual thinkers, researchers, and product builders.

Visual Canvas
Drag-and-drop mind mapping with infinite canvas, smart connections, and real-time undo/redo.
YouTube Integration
Embed any YouTube video and capture timestamped nodes. Jump back to any moment in one click.
Invite & Collaborate
Invite teammates by their unique Kexo UID. Role-based access: owner, editor, or viewer.
Share Links
Generate a public read-only link to share your workspace with anyone — no account needed.
Media Uploads
Attach images, files, and links. Record voice notes directly in the workspace. All stored via Cloudinary.
AI Assistant Soon
An AI that helps you summarize notes, generate mind map nodes, and answer questions — coming soon.
