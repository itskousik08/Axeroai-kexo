The Problem

Your ideas are scattered. Your tools don't connect them.

Notes Everywhere

Scattered across apps, notebooks and browser tabs. No cohesion, no structure, no way to find what you need.

No Context

Linear notes miss the relationships between ideas. The connections in your mind can't be captured with bullets.

Research is Slow

Re-reading old notes to rediscover what you already knew. Rebuilding context every time you return.

The Solution

A canvas that thinks the way you do

kexo AI gives you an infinite visual space to lay out your ideas, draw connections, and build a living map of your knowledge — one project at a time.

  • Drag, connect and rearrange ideas freely
  • Multiple node types: concepts, questions, notes, images
  • Organized into projects — your thinking, structured
  • Everything saved locally — no account needed

Features

Everything you need to think deeply

Project Workspace

Organize your thinking into projects. Each project gets its own infinite canvas, saved and accessible anytime.

Infinite Canvas

Pan and zoom freely across an unlimited space. No restrictions on how big your thinking can grow.

Visual Connections

Draw bezier curves between nodes to represent relationships. Unlink them just as easily.

Rich Node Types

Concepts, questions, notes and images — each with editable title, description and color coding.

Private by Default

Everything is stored in your browser's localStorage. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data leaving your device.

Instant & Fast

No loading screens, no login, no friction. Open and start thinking in under a second.
